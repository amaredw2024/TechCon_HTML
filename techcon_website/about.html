Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of technology enthusiasts and developers who wanted to share innovative ideas and discuss the future of software development. Over the years, it has grown into one of the most anticipated annual tech conferences, attracting thousands of attendees from around the globe.

Our Mission

The mission of TechCon 2024 is to inspire innovation, foster collaboration, and empower individuals to create technology that makes the world a better place. We believe in bringing together minds from diverse fields to share knowledge, build networks, and push the boundaries of what technology can achieve.

Past Speakers

TechCon has hosted some of the brightest minds in technology over the years. Here are a few of them:

Dr. Ada Lovelace

Recognized as the world’s first computer programmer, Dr. Lovelace’s groundbreaking insights continue to inspire generations of coders and innovators.

Alan Turing

The father of modern computing and artificial intelligence, Turing’s legacy shapes how we think about logic, computation, and technology.

Grace Hopper

A pioneer of computer programming languages, Grace Hopper’s work led to the creation of COBOL and inspired countless developers to dream big.
